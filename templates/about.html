About OptiCrop

Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine

Project Purpose

OptiCrop is an advanced, data-driven software system that utilizes Machine Learning to optimize agricultural production for different crops. By integrating key environmental factors such as Nitrogen (N), Phosphorous (P), Potassium (K) levels, soil temperature, humidity, pH, rainfall, and crop types, OptiCrop provides intelligent recommendations to farmers for maximizing yields and resource efficiency.

The knowledge and insights gained through OptiCrop can be utilized by agricultural researchers, policymakers, and stakeholders to advance understanding of crop-environment interactions and inform evidence-based agricultural strategies.

Scenarios Covered

  • Scenario 1 - Smart Crop Recommendation: Farmers enter soil & environmental details and receive AI-recommended crops for maximum yield.
  • Scenario 2 - Crop Suitability & Environmental Assessment: Users check whether current conditions suit a specific crop they have in mind.
  • Scenario 3 - Agricultural Research & Policy Planning: Researchers analyze crop-environment relationships through interactive visual dashboards.

How the ML Pipeline Works

  1. Dataset of 2,200 samples across 22 crops is generated/loaded (N, P, K, temperature, humidity, ph, rainfall).
  2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is performed using Pandas, Matplotlib & Seaborn.
  3. Features are scaled using StandardScaler; crop labels are encoded using LabelEncoder.
  4. Data is split 80/20 into training and testing sets.
  5. A RandomForestClassifier (300 trees) is trained on the training set.
  6. The model is evaluated using accuracy, a classification report, and a confusion matrix.
  7. The trained model, scaler, and encoder are saved with joblib for the Flask backend to load and serve predictions.
